Go Neutral

This React web application makes it incredibly easy for users to reduce their carbon footprint. I've set up the project's structure and layout. The user enters their zip code and whether or not they want to offset their individual or household's footprint. Those two inputs are set in state, then used to determine their emissions, their emissions after getting renewable energy, which renewable energy providers are available to them, and where their energy comes from. This is a personal project, in progress. Currently average US emissions are hard-coded in and I will be adding the needed data soon.
