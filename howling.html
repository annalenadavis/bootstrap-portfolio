Howling at the Edge of Chaos

I designed and coded this wordpress site for a friend who owns a small shop in Beacon, New York. The shop is filled with a rotating collection of highly curated artwork, clothing, jewelry, and other items created by all female artisans.

Project Details

  • Responsive design
  • Custom wordpress theme
  • PHP, HTML5, CSS3
  • JavaScript animation
See the Code

I developed two design ideas, each featuring the business' logo, and worked with the client to choose the direction for the final design. The store's location and hours are the first and last things customers will see. And the site features the shop's instagram feed highlighting the shop's products which change on a regular basis.
